# Intelligence and Octagon Verdict flow

- Intelligence is opened from the persistent header Ask control, not primary navigation.
- Primary navigation remains Home, Rankings, Play, and Picks until permission-aware War Room ownership exists.
- Fighter profile Compare opens Intelligence with that fighter selected and the opponent field focused.
- Fighter profile Ask Why copies a current calculated-rank question and opens the visible context card.
- Matchup prompts stay concise; Octagon Verdict owns verdict structure and deeper answer guidance.
- Copy & Open Verdict is the primary action, with visible prompt text and a separate copy fallback.
